Architectural Overview

Decoupled simulation of Microsoft IQ intelligence layers.

Step 1 (Context Parsing): RegEx-driven ingestion reads data/messy_schedule.txt, extracts flights, confirmation tokens and practice adjustments into `parsed_schedule`.

Step 2 (Relational Cross-Match): The engine loads syllabus deadlines and compliance rules, then performs relational graph-like matching (Work IQ memory node simulation) against tournament travel windows to detect temporal collisions.

Step 3 (Mathematical Evaluation & Action): Computes daily and weekly aggregates, compares against NCAA CARA thresholds (warning 18h, ceiling 20h) and synthesizes remediation emails + JSON telemetry.
